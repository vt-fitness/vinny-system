# Vinny Script Skill v1 — Multi-Angle Creative

## CONTEXT — Vinny's brand

**Audience primary:** Nam Việt-American 25-45, US (Cali/Texas/Florida prim)
- Nghề: nail tech / IT (đặc biệt ca đêm) / restaurant / văn phòng IT / skilled trades
- Family: thường có vợ con, làm 2 jobs, ngủ 5-6h
- Body state: skinny-fat, bụng bia, mất shape sau 30
- Mindset: muốn lấy lại body nhưng sợ mất cuộc sống / không có time / không tin promise quick fix

**Vinny's voice:**
- "Người anh đi trước" — đã từng skinny-fat, đã transform, giờ giúp anh em khác
- 7+ năm coaching experience (LA Fitness + private clients)
- Mình/bạn (NEVER tao/mày)
- Code-switching tiếng Anh OK khi natural cho Việt-American context
- KHÔNG dùng từ "Việt kiều" — dùng nghề/hoàn cảnh cụ thể

**3 pillars:** Mind / Nutrition / Training

**Growth Goals 2026:** 413k → 570k followers EOY

## STEP 1 — DETECT MODE

Read user input + idea context để detect priority:

**MODE: Growth (default 70%)**
- Pure value content, no coaching CTA
- Soft CTA: "save", "share with anh em", community building
- Goal: viral potential, broader appeal

**MODE: Conversion (default 30%)**
- Soft DM coaching CTA OK (natural integration, not pushy)
- Goal: turn warm followers → discovery call → coaching client
- Trigger phrases: when idea explicit về meal plan, training plan, transformation

**MODE: Hybrid**
- Default when ambiguous
- Lean 70/30 across the script body

## STEP 2 — ANALYZE THE IDEA

Before generating, evaluate the idea against:

**Hook strength:** What's the pattern interrupt? What hooks attention in 3s?

**Pillar fit:** Mind / Nutrition / Training / Story / Culture
- Each pillar has different best-fit angles
- Mind/Story → vulnerability + narrative work well
- Nutrition/Training → mechanism reveal + specific advice work
- Culture → cultural moments + family dynamics

**Audience pain depth:** Surface-level vs deep struggle?
- Deep pain → conversion-friendly
- Surface pain → growth-friendly

**Production effort:** 1-3
- 1 = Talking head (low effort, fast turnaround)
- 2 = B-roll + voiceover (medium)
- 3 = Cinematic / multi-scene / props (high effort)

## STEP 3 — GENERATE 2-3 ANGLES

Generate 2-3 different creative approaches for the same idea. Each angle should use a different lens. Pick from these creative levers:

### Storytelling angles
- **Personal vulnerability** — Vinny's own struggle (chỉ dùng stories thật)
- **Customer transformation** — student's journey
- **Day-in-life slice** — relatable moment
- **Behind-the-scenes** — show work
- **Failure-then-lesson** — vulnerability + insight

### Direct response angles
- **Pattern interrupt + bold claim** — counter-conventional opener
- **Mechanism reveal** — TẠI SAO behind WHAT
- **Mythbusting** — debunk common belief
- **"If you want X, do Y"** — direct framework
- **Specific advice** — "3 thứ phải làm sau ca 12h"

### Cultural angles (Việt-American specific)
- **Family dynamics** — vợ chia sẻ cho chồng (viral loop), cha mẹ vs mình
- **Workplace context** — nail salon culture, IT remote, restaurant grind, immigrant work ethic
- **Generation gap** — first-gen vs second-gen perspective
- **Cultural code-switching** — moments only Việt-American get

### Visceral angles
- **POV / silent film** — visual-driven, minimal voiceover
- **Visual metaphor** — abstract concept, concrete image
- **Reaction / commentary** — leverage existing trend (no creator attacks)

### Hybrid combos (Vinny's signature)
- Cultural pain + mechanism reveal + soft conversion
- Personal story + mythbusting + community CTA
- Vulnerability + authority + invitation to coaching

## CREATIVE CONSTRAINTS — Hard rules

These are non-negotiable. Apply to ALL angles generated:

### Voice & tone
- ✅ Mình/bạn (always)
- ✅ "Người anh đi trước" framing
- ✅ Code-switching English OK if natural
- ❌ Tao/mày (anywhere, ever)
- ❌ "Chuyên gia" / "expert" tone
- ❌ Generic "anh em ơi"

### Audience targeting
- ✅ Specific: "anh em làm nail / IT đêm / restaurant / văn phòng / 2 jobs / có vợ con"
- ❌ "Việt kiều" anywhere
- ❌ Vague "mọi người"

### Authority anchoring (when relevant)
- ✅ "7 năm coaching", "100+ anh em đã transform"
- ✅ Specific experience claims with proof
- ❌ Vague "anh từng thấy", "có người nói"

### Stories
- ✅ Stories THẬT từ Vinny's background
- ✅ Third-person student stories (with permission)
- ❌ Bịa stories — never invent skinny-fat stories, transformation timelines

### Hook structure
- Hook giây 0-3 PHẢI có ≥ 3/4 components:
  1. Visual cue cụ thể
  2. Verbal hook < 12 chữ
  3. Text on screen
  4. Sound/music vibe

### CTA strategy by mode
- Growth mode: NO "DM coaching". Soft CTA = "save", "share with anh em", "comment"
- Conversion mode: Soft "DM 'COACHING'" or "link in bio" — natural, not pushy
- Hybrid: Default value-first, soft conversion at very end

### Content boundaries
- ❌ Politics
- ❌ Religion
- ❌ Finance flex
- ❌ Women stereotypes / anti-rhetoric
- ❌ Health misinformation
- ❌ Personal attacks on creators
- ❌ Quick fix promises (1 tuần 10kg, etc.)

## ANTI-PATTERNS — Đừng generate scripts:

- ❌ All 3 angles the same approach (defeat purpose of multi-angle)
- ❌ Rhetorical questions opener ("Bạn có biết không...?")
- ❌ Generic motivation ("never give up", "hustle harder")
- ❌ Round numbers not believable ("1000 anh em")
- ❌ Hooks không có pattern interrupt (boring openers)
- ❌ Solutions không có mechanism (just tips, no WHY)
- ❌ CTA "DM COACHING" trong growth-mode scripts
- ❌ Scripts cụt — phải đủ structure 6 layers (hook → context → mechanism → proof → solution → close)
